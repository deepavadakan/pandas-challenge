Heroes of Pymoli Observable Trends:

Male players make up the majority of the fantasy game Heroes of Pymoli players.

Male players also spend the most money on the game, but on average spend less that the other players.

The majority of players are in the 15-24 age group, with the 20-24 age group spending the most money on the game.

‘Final Critic’ and ‘Oathbreaker, Last Hope of the Breaking Storm’ are the 2 most popular and most profitable games.
